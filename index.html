HarvestKeeper Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

HarvestKeeper is developed and operated by ArcLink Tech ("we," "us," "our"). This policy explains what information HarvestKeeper collects, how it is used, and what control you have over it.

Who this applies to

This policy covers the HarvestKeeper mobile app, used by pastors and soul winners, and its companion administrative tool used by ArcLink Tech to manage church registrations.

Information we collect

Account and registration information: church name, church location, pastor's name, phone number, and email address; soul winners' names, phone numbers, and email addresses.

Ministry records ("souls"): names, phone numbers, locations, discipleship status, follow-up notes, and related fields that pastors and soul winners choose to enter about the people they minister to.

Device information: a device identifier used to link your device to your church account and license.

Security credentials: PINs are hashed before storage and are never stored or transmitted in plain text.

Payment information: HarvestKeeper does not collect or store your card or Mobile Money details. Payments are processed entirely by Paystack, a third-party payment processor. HarvestKeeper only receives confirmation that a payment succeeded or failed, along with the amount and date.

Google Drive backup (optional): if you choose to connect your Google account, HarvestKeeper creates a single backup file inside your own Google Drive, in a folder the app creates. This uses Google's drive.file permission scope, which only allows the app to access files it has created itself — HarvestKeeper cannot see, read, or modify any other files, folders, or content in your Google Drive. Backups exclude profile photos to keep backup size small.

How we use this information

  • To operate the app: registering churches, managing soul winner access, and displaying ministry records back to the people who entered them.
  • To process subscription payments, via Paystack.
  • To send you notifications relevant to your account (such as trial or subscription status) if you have enabled notifications.
  • To provide customer support when you contact us.
  • To allow you to back up and restore your own data via your own Google Drive, if you choose to connect it.

We do not sell your information, and we do not use it for advertising.

Who can access your information

You control your own church's ministry records, soul winner list, and account settings directly through the app.

HarvestKeeper administrators (ArcLink Tech staff) do not access individual soul records in the course of normal operation. Administrators retain the operational access necessary to run the platform — for example, reviewing church registrations, managing subscription status, and providing customer support — and may access account-level information (such as church name, contact details, and payment status) as needed for those purposes. Administrators do not routinely view the contents of soul records entered by pastors or soul winners.

Third-party services we rely on to operate HarvestKeeper:

  • Supabase — our database and backend infrastructure provider, which stores your account and ministry data.
  • Paystack — our payment processor, which handles all subscription payments.
  • Google — only if you choose to connect Google Drive for backup, and only for the single backup file the app creates.

We do not share your information with any other third party except where required by law.

Data security

Data is transmitted between the app and our servers over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. PINs are hashed before storage, not stored in plain text. Ministry and account data is stored using Supabase's standard infrastructure and security practices. No method of storage or transmission is completely immune to risk, and we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable, standard measures to protect your information.

Your choices and control

  • You may update or delete your church's records and soul winner accounts directly within the app.
  • You may disconnect Google Drive backup at any time; doing so does not delete your existing backup file, which remains in your own Google Drive under your control.
  • You may contact us to request deletion of your church's account and associated data.

Children's information

HarvestKeeper is intended for use by adults (pastors and soul winners) managing ministry records. If a soul record entered by a pastor or soul winner pertains to a minor, that information is provided and controlled by the person who entered it, as part of their own ministry record-keeping, and is treated with the same access controls as any other soul record.

International use

HarvestKeeper is used by churches in multiple countries, including across Africa, North America, and elsewhere. By using the app, you consent to your information being processed and stored on infrastructure that may be located outside your own country.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date above.
